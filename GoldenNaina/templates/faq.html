What is Myntra's Shipping Fee?

a) Shipping fee is charged towards providing delivery and post order services including but not limited to, higher than average returns

b) Shipping is a value added service charge levied under one of the following circumstances:

  1. Orders below a certain value. The value can vary based on the category
  2. a) Orders below INR 1199 for all categories except personal care and personal care orders below INR 299 (not applicable for Insiders) are charged with Shipping fee.
  3. b) The final shipping fee is calculated post application of any 'coupon benefit' and offers
  4. c) The threshold values can change, please review the Shipping Fee at the time of checkout
  5. c) Shipping fee is not levied to Myntra Insiders, irrespective of the order value.
