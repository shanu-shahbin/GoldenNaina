Introduction

At Golden Naina, we deeply value the trust you place in us, and we recognize the immense responsibility that comes with safeguarding your personal information. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and ensuring that your personal data is secure with us. Our mission is to provide a seamless and personalized shopping experience for all our customers, whether you're exploring the latest trends or discovering timeless classics.

You also agree to be governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates (UAE), including but not limited to laws concerning data protection and privacy. If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, we kindly request that you refrain from using our Platform.

Information We Collect

We collect and process various types of personal data to provide you with a customized and efficient shopping experience. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Identity and Contact Information: This includes your name, date of birth, gender, address, telephone/mobile number, and email ID. This information is essential for account creation, order processing, and communication with you regarding your orders and our services.
  • Payment Information: We collect details related to your payment methods, including credit or debit card information, bank account details, or other payment instruments. This sensitive information is processed securely in accordance with applicable legal requirements and is only used to complete transactions on our Platform.
  • Shopping Preferences: We collect data on your shopping behavior, preferences, and browsing history to personalize your experience on our Platform. This may include information about the products you view, purchase, or add to your wishlist.
  • Device and Location Information: We may collect information about the devices you use to access our Platform, including IP address, browser type, and operating system. We may also collect your device's location to provide location-based services and enhance your shopping experience.
  • Biometric Data: With your consent, we may collect biometric data, such as facial features or physiological information, to enable advanced features on our Platform, such as virtual try-ons or secure authentication.
  • Communication Data: This includes records of your communications with us, whether through our customer service channels, email, or social media platforms. This information helps us improve our services and address your concerns more effectively.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing and Fulfillment: Your personal data is used to process and fulfill your orders, communicate with you about your purchases, and ensure that your products are delivered to you in a timely manner.
  • Personalization: We use your data to personalize your shopping experience, recommend products, and tailor our Platform to meet your specific preferences. This includes providing you with personalized content, promotions, and offers.
  • Customer Support: We use your information to provide customer support, address your inquiries, resolve issues, and improve our services based on your feedback.
  • Marketing and Promotions: With your consent, we may use your contact information to send you marketing communications, including newsletters, promotional offers, and updates about new products and services. You have the option to opt-out of these communications at any time.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: We use your data to protect our Platform and users from fraud, unauthorized access, and other illegal activities. This includes monitoring for suspicious activities and ensuring the security of your transactions.
  • Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with applicable legal obligations, respond to legal requests, and enforce our terms and conditions.
  • Research and Development: We may use your data to conduct research, analysis, and business intelligence to improve our products, services, and overall customer experience. This includes analyzing trends, preferences, and behaviors to better understand our customers.

Our Platform may contain links to other websites that are not operated by Golden Naina. These third-party sites may collect personal data about you. Please be aware that Golden Naina is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those linked websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit to understand how they collect, use, and share your information.

Security Precautions

To protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, loss, or misuse, we adopt reasonable security practices and procedures, in line with international standards such as ISO/IEC 27001:2013, which is recognized as one of the highest information security standards. Whenever you access your account information, we offer the use of a secure server.

However, please be aware that the transmission of information over the internet is not completely secure due to reasons beyond our control. While we adopt industry-standard security measures and regularly update our systems to protect your personal data against hacking or virus dissemination, by using the Platform, you accept the security implications of data transmission over the internet and the World Wide Web. There are inherent risks that cannot be completely eliminated, and we advise users to take precautions, such as ensuring the protection of login and password records for their account.

Choice/Opt-Out

We provide all users with the opportunity to opt-out of receiving non-essential (promotional, marketing-related) communications from us after setting up an account. If you do not wish to receive promotional communications from us, you can unsubscribe by clicking on the unsubscribe link in the email or visit our Help Center here to unsubscribe/opt-out.

Advertisements

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our Platform. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this Platform and other websites to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. You have the option to opt-out from tracking of personalized advertising using the “Opt out of Ads Personalization” settings in your device’s settings application. Golden Naina will have no access to your GAID once you select this feature.

Use of our Platform is available only to persons who can form a legally binding contract under the applicable laws of the UAE. If you have shared any personal data of children under the age of 18 years, you represent that you have the authority to do so and permit us to use the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Data Deletion and Retention

You have the option to delete your account by visiting your Profile and Settings on our Platform. This action will result in the loss of all information related to your account. You can also write to us at the contact information provided below to assist you with these requests. Please note that deletion of your account may prevent you from accessing your order history, preferences, and other account-related information, including pending orders, exchanges, returns, refunds, coupons, or loyalty program benefits.

In certain cases, such as pending grievances, claims, or services, we may refuse or delay the deletion of your account. Deletion of the account will not be retroactive and will be in accordance with this Privacy Policy, related Terms of Use, and applicable laws. Once the account is deleted, you will need to register as a new user to access the Platform again. We retain your personal data for a period no longer than required for the purposes for which it was collected or as required under any applicable law. However, we may retain data related to you if we believe it may be necessary to prevent fraud or future abuse, to enable Golden Naina to exercise its legal rights and/or defend against legal claims, or for other legitimate purposes. We may continue to retain your data in anonymized form for analytical and research purposes.
